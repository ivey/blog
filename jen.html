why jeniffer garner?

Poem for Jen was originally published under the name Jeniffer Garner as Beef Stew. In fact, she submitted it to her English class as her own work, with my permission. That's why I wrote the poem. I liked it, she liked it, and her class loved it. She won 3 awards at her school with it. Boy that amuses me.

So anyway, this is just sort of a "I didn't steal it, she didn't steal it, I wrote it, she published it under her name, and now it's back under my name" kind of disclaimer. Enjoy.
